About Us

Our history

In the early 1990s, Australia and New Zealand had an urgent need for doctors. A need we were born to fill in the form of an unprecedented business. Since then, we’ve successfully matched thousands of doctors with clients around the world.

Our position

Today, we place doctors in New Zealand, Australia, Guam, the Caribbean, Canada, China, and of course, the US. Historically, we’ve had doctors in Singapore and the United Arab Emirates — and we regularly expand into new countries. This worldwide reach means that if a location is placeable, we’re probably there. And it makes us unique among locum tenens agencies.

Our advantage(s)

Our reach is global. But our service is personal. And our size allows us to move with both the speed and attention to detail our doctors and clients deserve.

Our purpose

Thanks to the opportunities and support we provide, doctors can experience the world and practice medicine in ways that benefit themselves and their patients. And through the Making a Difference Funding Programm, we give physicians additional opportunities to help where it’s needed most — in the U.S. and abroad.

HOW WE MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Our doctors

Exceptional physicians are a huge part of who we are. And they tend to be a different breed. Many have worked in underserved populations, seek out unique or challenging assignments, and, in general, are more flexible and adaptable.
